## A.3 Involvement of Human Participants

**Does this project involve human participants?** No.

This project is a computer-science engineering project. All data used in evaluation are synthetically generated AWS Lambda telemetry records. No individuals were recruited, surveyed, interviewed, observed, or otherwise involved as research subjects. The evaluation corpus was produced programmatically and contains no personally identifiable information.

**Why are human participants not involved?** The research objective is to design and evaluate a software artefact — the SCAFAD anomaly detection framework — against quantitative performance metrics. The research questions (RQ1–RQ3) are answered through measurement of the system, not through elicitation from people.
